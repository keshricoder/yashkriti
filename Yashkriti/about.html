Our Story

“Our mission is to connect Colombia’s top talent with US companies and startups, nurturing mutual growth.”
- Andres Max, Founder
In 2010 our Founder, Andres Max, opened Ideaware’s doors to the US market. What started as a UX/UI design agency for startups in Silicon Valley transitioned to a dedicated nearshore IT staff augmentation firm.

We reinvented ourselves.

Eleven years later, we keep connecting Colombia’s top IT talent to US companies and starups. Every day, we make sure to keep our team happy and motivated to share their talent, passion, and energy with our partners and their projects.

Colombia is ranked the third Tech Hub in Latin America. Our IT Industry is a fast-growing and innovative market, with a significant workforce. Over USD 8.2 billion were invested in the sector in 2019.

Our HQ is located in Barranquilla, where most of the team lives. The office is always open to our team whenever they need/want to work from there. However, we continue working remotely and hiring IT talent throughout the country.
